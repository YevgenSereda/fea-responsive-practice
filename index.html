brixx x trends 2017

more versatility, more freedom

BRIXX – our very first fully upholstered collection, developed in collaboration with Lorenza Bozzoli. Its comfortable shapes and flexible system of rectangular modules enable a wonderfully playful 360° experience. Whether you are relaxing, spending time with your friends or it's just the two with you – BRIXX combines famous DEDON quality with striking colours, shapes and design to create an adaptable system to suit everyone’s lifestyle.

LORENZA BOZZOLI and her eye for design and detail: Her career started as a fashion designer for international labels such as Sergio Rossi, Plein Sud and Fiorucci. Today she works as a successful product and furniture designer at her own atelier in Milan – one of the world’s design capitals.

Lorenza’s ongoing collaboration began with the PORCINI collection, followed by the FEDRO chair, which was awarded the EESC European Award for Sustainable Design. Now we are introducing BRIXX, a fully upholstered collection of modules in distinctive, eye-catching colours.

By combining the independent pieces and accessories you can create endless set-ups, outdoors as well as in. Enjoy moments of laisser-faire.
